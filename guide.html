Carolina Beach Events In 2022

Carolina Beach Boardwalk


Enjoy the sights and sounds of Carolina Beach when you visit the Carolina Beach Boardwalk. Located oceanfront, this popular hub for fun and excitement boasts a Ferris wheel, arcade, restaurants, shopping and seasonal concerts & festivals.

North Carolina Aquarium at Fort Fisher

Discover exotic creatures from the land and sea at the North Carolina Aquarium at Fort Fisher. Exhibits include animals from freshwater streams and swamps to fish from tropical coral reefs from around the world.

Carolina Beach Fishing Pier


Carolina Beach State Park


Freeman Park


reeman Park is a stretch of beautiful Carolina Beach where 4×4 wheels are permitted to drive onto the beach for boating, fishing, camping, surfing, swimming and more. Visitors love to see the variety of wildlife and plant life along the beaches and dunes that are indigenous to the area.
